Fabriq is a liquidity and market-intelligence tool for Liquidity Providers and active traders on Solana.

It offers real-time visibility into liquidity pools, LP positions, fees, and performance using data indexed directly from on-chain activity. Updates in real-time as market conditions change.

Fabriq brings pool discovery, performance tracking, and position management into a single interface. It is built natively for Solana and supports advanced liquidity architectures such as DLMM and DAMM.
