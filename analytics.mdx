Analytics provides a market-wide view of liquidity activity across supported Solana liquidity programs.

## Overview

Analytics includes summary metrics and a trend chart for the selected programs and time range.

At the top of the page, the summary cards show:

- Liquidity movement (24h)
- Fees (24h)
- Unique wallets (24h)
- Opened positions (24h)

These metrics update based on the selected program filters.

## Program Filters

Users can switch between:

- All
- DLMM
- DAMM V2
- CLMM
- CPMM
- AMM V4
- AMM

## Analytics Trend

The Analytics Trend chart shows aggregated activity across the selected markets in UTC.

Users can switch between:

- 30D
- 6M

The chart is displayed as a stacked bar view so users can compare where activity is coming from across the selected programs.

## Metric Views

The trend chart can be switched between:

- Liquidity movement
- Fees
- Unique wallets
- Opened positions
- Closed positions

## What It Shows

Analytics gives users a broader view of on-chain LP activity by surfacing:

- Liquidity movement across supported programs
- Fee generation across selected markets
- Unique wallet participation
- Position opening activity
- Position closing activity
