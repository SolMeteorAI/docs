title: Sol Reclaim
description: 'Recover SOL from empty token accounts in one flow'
---

This feature is available on both:

- Trending Pools
- Portfolio

## Requirements

- Wallet must be connected
- Connected wallet must contain empty token accounts
- Reclaim only applies to empty token accounts owned by that connected wallet

## How To

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Click the displayed empty token accounts reclaim button.
3. Reclaim.
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

After confirmation, reclaimed SOL is sent back to your wallet.

- Fabriq charges a `10%` fee on SOL reclaims.
- Final reclaim amount is shown before confirmation.
