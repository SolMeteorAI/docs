Learn how to navigate Fabriq's tools and get the most out of the platform.

Fabriq provides a streamlined set of features designed to help traders and liquidity providers explore pool activity, monitor performance, and manage LP positions with clarity and speed. This guide explains where key features are located and how to use them effectively.

## Accessing Trending Pools

The Trending tab allows you to discover the most active pools across Meteora's DLMM and DAMM.

You can:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pool Discovery" icon="chart-line">
    **→ View top-performing pools**  
    **→ Inspect pool metrics before entering a position**
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Filtering & Sorting" icon="filter">
    **→ Sort and filter by volume, liquidity**  
    **→ Filter by fees per minute, trades per minute**  
    **→ Sort by APR and MeteorScore**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**Trending Pools gives traders and LPs a real-time view of market activity.**
</Info>

## Viewing Your Portfolio

Connecting your wallet unlocks the Portfolio tab, where Fabriq automatically organizes your liquidity activity.

The Portfolio includes:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Active Positions" icon="chart-bar">
    View your current LP positions with real-time PnL, fees, and available actions.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="History" icon="clock">
    A timeline of past LP actions including opens, closes, claims, and compounds.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Transactions" icon="list">
    A filtered view of wallet transactions relevant to Fabriq and Meteora interactions.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Balances" icon="wallet">
    View your current wallet token balances and total value.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**This gives users an at-a-glance overview of position values, fees, and wallet activity.**
</Info>

## Managing Liquidity Positions

Within the Active Positions section, users can:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Position Controls" icon="gear">
    **→ Claim rewards**  
    **→ Compound (where supported)**  
    **→ Close positions**  
    **→ Zap-Out**
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Analytics & Sharing" icon="chart-line">
    **→ View LP-based PnL**  
    **→ Download PnL Cards**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**Fabriq consolidates position management into a single, intuitive interface.**
</Info>

## Using Filters and MeteorScore

**Filters allow you to refine pool searches based on your strategy.**

**MeteorScore helps identify active pools more quickly.**

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Custom Filters" icon="sliders">
    Refine searches by APR, volume, liquidity, fees per minute, trades per minute, and more.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="MeteorScore" icon="target">
    Activity-based scoring that helps identify pools gaining traction and showing strong market conditions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**These tools help users focus on pools that align with their objectives.**
</Info>

## Sol-Reclaim

Fabriq identifies empty token accounts and lets you reclaim the SOL locked inside.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="One-Click Process" icon="bolt">
    A single click initiates the process, helping maintain a clean and efficient wallet.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Automatic Detection" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Fabriq automatically scans and identifies all empty token accounts associated with your wallet.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
**Fee Structure**: A small 10% fee applies to cover transaction costs and platform upkeep.
</Warning>

## Zap-Out

Fabriq simplifies exiting LP positions through integrated Zap-Out functionality, converting liquidity back into a single asset.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Simple Exit" icon="door-open">
    Exit your position and convert liquidity back into SOL, USDC, or the farming token in one action.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Integrated Workflow" icon="link">
    Zap-Out is seamlessly integrated into the position management interface for quick access.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**No need to switch between platforms — manage your entire LP lifecycle in one place.**
</Info>
