Master Fabriq's filtering tools and learn how the MeteorScore ranking system helps identify active and efficient pools.

Fabriq provides a detailed set of filters and customizable columns that allow users to refine the Trending Pools table based on liquidity, activity, volatility, and performance metrics. These tools give traders and LPs a clear view of pools that match their strategy.

## Filters

All filtering options are available in the Table Settings panel under the "Filters" tab. Each filter supports a minimum, maximum, or both inputs.

## MeteorScore

The MeteorScore highlights a pool's efficiency and momentum relative to its liquidity.

It reflects how active, efficient, and fee-generating a pool is across recent timeframes.

### MeteorScore considers four primary factors:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Trading Activity" icon="chart-candlestick">
    **Recent trading activity**  
    Measures swaps and trading frequency across multiple short-term windows.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="LP Activity" icon="droplet">
    **Recent LP activity**  
    Captures liquidity movements, migrations, and position adjustments by LPs.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Fee Generation" icon="coins">
    **Fees paid to liquidity providers**  
    Evaluates fee generation relative to time and activity.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Liquidity Depth" icon="waves">
    **Liquidity**  
    Considers the depth and distribution of available liquidity in the pool.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**Higher scores suggest stronger, more balanced short-term activity with better potential opportunity for liquidity providers.**
</Info>
