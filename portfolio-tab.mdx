Your complete view of liquidity positions, wallet activity, and performance on Solana.

The Portfolio Tab organizes all of your LP-related data into a single, clean dashboard. It provides visibility into your active positions, historical activity, wallet balances, and transaction records, helping both traders and liquidity providers understand their performance at a glance.

## The Portfolio Tab is divided into four sections:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Active Positions" icon="chart-bar">
    View and manage your current LP positions with real-time PnL and available actions.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="History" icon="clock">
    A complete timeline of past LP operations and actions.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Transactions" icon="list">
    Filtered view of on-chain activity related to Fabriq and liquidity interactions.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Balances" icon="wallet">
    Complete snapshot of your wallet's token holdings and values.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**Each section updates automatically as you interact with pools and your connected wallet.**
</Info>

## Active Positions

The Active Positions page provides a full overview of the LP positions currently held by your connected wallet.

### Summary Panel

At the top of the page, Fabriq displays:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Portfolio Overview" icon="chart-pie">
    **→ Number of positions**  
    **→ Number of pools**  
    **→ Initial deposit value**  
    **→ Current deposit value**
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Performance Metrics" icon="chart-line">
    **→ Claimed fees**  
    **→ Unclaimed fees**  
    **→ Total PnL based on LP performance**  
    **→ Current deposit + unclaimed fees**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**This summary gives a fast, high-level view of your portfolio's standing and trajectory.**
</Info>

### Position-Level Details

Each active position includes:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Position Metrics" icon="chart-bar">
    **→ Current position value**  
    **→ Initial deposit**  
    **→ Unclaimed fees**  
    **→ LP-based PnL**
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Activity Data" icon="signal">
    **→ Fee breakdown**  
    **→ Time-based activity metrics**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Available Actions

Fabriq provides integrated controls for managing your liquidity:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Position Controls" icon="gear">
    **→ Claim rewards**  
    **→ Compound (if supported by the pool)**  
    **→ Close position**  
    **→ Zap-Out into a single asset**
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Analytics & Sharing" icon="image">
    **→ Generate a PnL Card** for sharing or recordkeeping
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**These actions allow you to adjust your positions directly from the dashboard without switching platforms.**
</Info>

## History

The History tab tracks all past LP operations performed by your wallet within supported pools.

### Recorded actions include:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Position Actions" icon="arrows-rotate">
    **→ Position opens**  
    **→ Position closes**  
    **→ Deposits**  
    **→ Withdrawals (where applicable)**
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Reward Actions" icon="coins">
    **→ Claims**  
    **→ Compounds**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**Each entry includes timestamped details, enabling full transparency and review of your liquidity activity.**
</Info>

<CardGroup cols={2}>

## Balances

The Balances tab displays all tokens currently held in your wallet along with:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Token Information" icon="coins">
    **→ Token name**  
    **→ Current price**  
    **→ FDV (when available)**
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Holdings" icon="wallet">
    **→ Amount**  
    **→ Total value**
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
**This offers a complete snapshot of your wallet's token holdings, including LP-related assets and idle balances.**
</Info>
