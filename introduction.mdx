A liquidity and market-intelligence tool for Liquidity Providers and active traders on Solana.

Real-time visibility into liquidity pools, LP positions, fees, and performance. Data is indexed directly from on-chain activity and updates continuously as market conditions change.

Pool discovery and performance tracking in a single interface designed for active liquidity workflows.

Built natively for Solana and supports advanced liquidity architectures such as DLMM and DAMM.

Discover active pools, manage positions, and reclaim stranded SOL — all from one interface.
