Fabriq is powered by an in-house indexer built to source liquidity data directly from the Solana blockchain.

We do not rely on third-party APIs for core liquidity data. Instead, we index activity ourselves so the product is built on a direct on-chain source of truth.

![Fabriq unifying liquidity provision across Solana DEXs into one interface.](/images/fabriq-dex-indexed.png)

## What Makes Fabriq Different

Fabriq is built around direct blockchain indexing rather than external data vendors.

- Direct on-chain sourcing instead of third-party API dependence
- One internal data model across multiple DEX environments
- A foundation for both LP discovery and LP management in one interface

This allows Fabriq to unify fragmented liquidity into a single product experience instead of treating each DEX as a separate workflow.

## What We Index

The indexer processes liquidity data across the 6 main DEXes on Solana.

It covers the underlying liquidity, activity, and position data needed to standardize fragmented DEX environments into one usable product layer.

Because this data is indexed in-house, Fabriq can normalize it across DEXs and expose it through one interface instead of forcing you to compare fragmented tools and inconsistent datasets.

## Why It Matters

This indexing layer powers:

- Cross-DEX liquidity discovery in one place
- Unified portfolio tracking and PnL analysis
- A shared foundation for future cross-DEX LP management

It gives Fabriq control over data quality, coverage, and product direction, and it is what makes a more unified cross-DEX liquidity experience possible on Solana.
