The Sol Reclaim page explains how users can recover SOL from empty token accounts.

This feature is available in both:

- Trending Pools tab
- Portfolio tab

## Requirements

- Wallet must be connected
- Connected wallet must contain empty token accounts
- Reclaim only applies to empty token accounts owned by that connected wallet

## How to Reclaim SOL

1. Click the displayed empty token accounts reclaim button.
2. Select `Claim`.
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

After confirmation, reclaimed SOL is sent back to your wallet.

## Fee

- Fabriq charges a `10%` fee on SOL reclaims.
- Final reclaim amount is shown before confirmation.

<Note>
You can view wallet and portfolio data without connecting, but reclaim actions require a connected wallet because they are on-chain transactions.
</Note>
