## 🔍 Step 2: Analyze Tokens

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Token Information">
    Enter a token name or address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Comprehensive Data">
    Fabriq returns real-time data, including:
    - **Price**
    - **Volume** 
    - **Fees**
    - **LP availability**
    - **FabriqScore** (pool quality metric)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Know what you're trading — before you move.**
</Tip>

## 🌊 Step 3: Explore Liquidity Pools

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search or Browse">
    Search or browse active pools
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Pool Data">
    Review pool-level data like:
    - **APR**
    - **Trading volume**
    - **Fee generation**
    - **Pool age**
    - **LP participation**
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your Positions">
    If your wallet is connected, you'll also see your active positions and rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>
