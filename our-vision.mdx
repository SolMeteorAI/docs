Unifying Liquidity Provision Across Solana

Brings together liquidity data from every major DEX and sits above individual protocols to show how liquidity moves, concentrates, and sticks across the ecosystem, so LPs can allocate capital with a complete view of Solana liquidity.

## The Problem

Each DEX exposes only its own pools. LPs are forced to jump between tools to understand where capital is deployed, how it moves, and which pools offer sustainable returns.
