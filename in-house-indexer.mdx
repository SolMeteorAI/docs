Fabriq is powered by an in-house indexer built to source liquidity data directly from the Solana blockchain.

We do not rely on third-party APIs for core liquidity data. Instead, we index activity ourselves so the product is built on the same on-chain source of truth that the market runs on.

That gives Fabriq the foundation for real-time updates, faster product iteration, and a consistent data layer across the platform.

## What Makes Fabriq Different

Fabriq is built around direct blockchain indexing rather than external data vendors.

- Direct on-chain sourcing instead of third-party API dependence
- Real-time updates as indexed activity changes on-chain
- One internal data model across multiple DEX environments
- A foundation for both LP discovery and LP management in one interface

This is what allows Fabriq to unify fragmented liquidity into a single product experience instead of treating each DEX as a separate workflow.

## What We Index

The indexer processes liquidity data across the 6 main DEXes on Solana.

This includes core liquidity and activity data such as:

- Pool state
- Liquidity changes
- Swaps
- Trading volume
- Fees
- Position activity and related historical records

Because this data is indexed in-house, Fabriq can standardize it across DEXs and expose it through one interface instead of forcing you to compare fragmented tools and inconsistent datasets.

## Why It Matters

Direct indexing is what makes the rest of Fabriq possible.

It powers:

- Cross-DEX liquidity discovery in one place
- Real-time market visibility
- Unified portfolio tracking and PnL analysis
- A shared foundation for future cross-DEX LP management

As Fabriq expands, this indexing layer is what allows the product to move toward cross-DEX LP discovery and management from a single interface.

## Product Foundation

Fabriq is not just a front end on top of someone else's data.

The in-house indexer is core infrastructure. It gives Fabriq control over data quality, speed, and coverage, and it is the reason the platform can deliver a more unified liquidity experience across Solana.
